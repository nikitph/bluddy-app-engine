The Problem

The world is not short of good intentions but its also not short of laziness. A lot more people would like to donate blood but they dont because of the hassle involved which in turn results in lost blood litres and hence lives

The Solution

By utilizing the power of cloud and our on ground volunteers, Bluddy is able to simplify blood donations so that you dont have to go anywhere. Bluddy volunteers come to you and take your blood. They are well trained phlebotomists. You can just relax and watch a movie in th comfort of your homes while you donate blood. Now that's simple

The first step

Just click on the get started button and follow the instructions. After you have been verified, you can then schedule an appointment. We thank you in advance for your great service to society.
